Home Small Network Wiring Project

29-09-2022

This is my first attempt at running Ethernet cables within the walls. Firstly, it's essential to prepare the wall by creating a space for the cables. Then, secure one end of the Ethernet cable and pull it through with force from the other end until the entire length is drawn out. Next, connect the cable to the panel. Finally, use a cable tester to ensure there are no breaks in the connection. This completes the process successfully.
