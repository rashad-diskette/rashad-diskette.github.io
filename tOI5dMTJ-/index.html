First Prize ! in the East China Division of World Educational Robot Cotest

29-03-2017

Guest Address:

The venue of the competition I am in:




Afterward, the newspaper interviewed us, and our achievements were featured in the newspaper.

Partial excerpt from the newspaper content:
"Wei Yubin, Li Zihao, and Han Runze participated in the Robot Skills Challenge. During the competition, they demonstrated excellent coordination and performed exceptionally well, ultimately securing the first prize in the event."

"The Robot Club received news of the competition on February 18th, with just over a month until the event. Considering the tight timeframe and the fact that most team members lacked prior competition experience, there was a sense of unease at the time," said Teacher Gao Liming to the Ezhou Evening News reporter. "Wei Yubin is the only member in the club with a foundation; he previously won a second prize at the 2016 International Olympic Robot Contest held in Inner Mongolia. The rest of the team members started learning from scratch in December of last year."

"After the competition, Wei Yubin's mother told the Yingzhou Evening News reporter that since her child joined the school's robotics club, they have become more attentive and independent than before. The ability to withstand setbacks and challenges has significantly improved. She is pleased to see such progress in her child and wholeheartedly supports his passion for robotics."
