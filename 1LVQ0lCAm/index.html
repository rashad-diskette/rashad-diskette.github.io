A super mini Laptop

15-03-2017

It's a laptop I purchased on a second-hand trading website, the ASUS EeePC 703 model. It comes with an 8GB solid-state drive, which operates very quietly. It has a wireless LAN card, and I upgraded the original 512MB RAM to a 2GB memory module. Its compact size, with just a 7-inch display, makes it extremely convenient for programming, no matter where I am.
